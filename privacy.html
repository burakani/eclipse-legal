Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

Eclipse ("we", "our", or "the app") is a PDF reader application for iOS. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data We Collect

Eclipse does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All PDF documents and reading preferences are stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service:

  • RevenueCat — Used to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase-related data such as transaction identifiers and subscription status. RevenueCat does not have access to your PDF documents or personal files. For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

All your data (PDF files, bookmarks, highlights, and preferences) is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is not backed up to our servers.

Analytics

Eclipse does not use any analytics or tracking tools.

Children's Privacy

Eclipse does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
