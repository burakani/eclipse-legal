Common Questions

How do I activate Dark Mode?

Tap the moon icon at the bottom-right of the PDF reader. Long-press it to choose between Pure Dark, Warm Dark, and Sepia themes.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings → Manage Subscription → Restore Purchases.

How do I redeem a promo code?

Go to Settings → Redeem Code, or tap "Redeem Code" on the premium upgrade screen.

Are my PDFs uploaded anywhere?

No. Eclipse processes everything on your device. Your documents never leave your iPhone.
